Pendragon,The Merchant Of Death

Bobby Pendragon is your ordinary 14 year old boy he plays sports and is pretty popular. But one day his Uncle Press tells him that he is a traveler, people who have to protect All the territories that make up Halla. Uncle Press takes him to a Flume basically a teleporter that transports people to different territories if they say the names of the Territories. Uncle Press Tells Bobby to say Denduron, a world that is in the stone age. Uncle Press informs him that all the territories are reaching a turning point in which they have to safe the worlds from Collapsing. Bobby gets to Denduron and Uncle Press gets Captured by the Bedowens the superior humans. There is another group, the Milago, The Bedowens treat the Milago like slaves. Bobby finds out that this man named Saint Dane is another traveler, But he does not do good he is trying to collapse all the Territories, Bobby must stop him and save Denduron and all the other Territories including Second Earth The one he is from.
